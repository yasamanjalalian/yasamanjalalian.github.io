Light Well Studio

Year
2023
Type
Workspace
Status
Completed

A deep floor plate is pierced by a single generous light well wrapped in pale plaster. The well anchors studio desks along its perimeter so models and drawings receive steady diffuse daylight from mid-morning through afternoon.

Storage integrates into wall thicknesses to keep the central volume quiet. At night, concealed perimeter fixtures graze texture instead of flooding the room—a deliberate setting for pin-up reviews.
