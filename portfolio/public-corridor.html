Public Corridor Proposal

Year
2023
Type
Infrastructure
Status
Study

Two civic buildings were linked by converting service lanes into a covered pedestrian corridor with alternating spans of glass and planted buffers. The sequence slows movement long enough for chance encounters without blocking emergency access routes.

Flooring shifts from rough granite at entries to finer grit concrete under the canopy, signaling transition from city-scale movement to campus-scale pause.
