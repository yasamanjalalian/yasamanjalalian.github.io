Coastal Fabric Atlas

Year
2024
Type
Urban research
Status
Publication

This atlas maps incremental coastal settlements as overlapping layers—sea defenses, informal paths, seasonal stalls, and reused industrial shells. Drawings pair aerial outlines with street-level transects to show how public space contracts and expands with tides.

The work proposes small architectural interventions that reinforce existing gathering spots rather than replacing them with singular landmarks.
