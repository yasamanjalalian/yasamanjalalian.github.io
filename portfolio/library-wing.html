Library Wing Renovation

Year
2024
Type
Adaptive reuse
Status
Built

The south wing’s former stacks became flexible reading rooms separated by acoustic curtains and low oak shelving. A continuous skylight band redirects daylight toward the interior corridor so the deepest part of the plan stays usable without harsh glare.

Radiant floors and discreet displacement ventilation keep mechanical noise away from quiet zones, while historical masonry remains exposed at thresholds between old and new construction.
