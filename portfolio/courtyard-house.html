Courtyard House Study

Year
2025
Type
Residential concept
Status
Design study

A compact courtyard organizes private rooms around a shared garden room. Deep roof eaves and rotated brick piers filter western sun while keeping the plan legible on a narrow urban lot.

Sectional overlap between kitchen and courtyard creates a shaded outdoor room usable for most of the year. Materials stay limited to lime plaster, timber, and ceramic paving so the architecture reads as a calm frame for plants and sky.
